Online Food Ordering

Order food from the school cafeteria easily. No need to queue in person. Fast, convenient, and efficient.

About Us

Objective

SmartCanteen aims to simplify the food ordering process in school canteens through a fast, practical, and efficient digital system, allowing students to order without having to queue in person.

Vision

To become a digital solution that simplifies food ordering in school canteens with a fast, practical, and efficient system, while improving service quality through the use of modern technology.

Mission

  • Provides a booking system that is easy for students to use.
  • Helping canteen vendors manage orders more efficiently.
  • Ensuring food quality and variety for a better canteen experience.

Features

Online Ordering

Order food directly from the app without having to queue at the canteen.

Order Status

View order status — processed, prepared, or ready to pick up — in real time.

Favorites Menu

Save your favorite menus for faster ordering in the future.

Order Cart

Manage orders before checkout with a convenient cart system.

Chat Seller

Communicate directly with the seller for menu or order information.

Digital Payments

Supports a variety of fast and secure digital payment methods.
